Operation Gatisheel Patna: Seamless Mobility
━━━━━━━━━▲━━━━━━━━━

Patna lacks adequate public transport system and faces perennial traffic congestion along the major roads, which leads to air and noise pollution. Non-conducive walking environment and encroachment by street vendors & unauthorized parking restricts mobility.
To ensure the last mile connectivity, Patna Smart City Plan has scope of an intelligent traffic and transport management systems to improve average speed and mobility. It encourages Non-Motorized Transport by allocating exclusive NMT streets. This will ensure equitable distribution of road space between all road users that is inclusive and accessible for all especially senior citizens and differently-abled.
